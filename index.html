Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more fo the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tabespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tabespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brue, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it is not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sixed mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You do not need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
